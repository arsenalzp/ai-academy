# EU AI Act Classification – EuroHealth AI Helpdesk

## HIGH-RISK (Category 4, Annex III)

EuroHealth AI Helpdesk falls under Annex III Category 4 because the system routes tickets, answers policy questions, and escalates issues, deployed across multiple EU member states, and delivering decision-support/decision-implicating actions to employees. 

By routing tickets, providing policy answers, and escalating issues, the system directly influences how work is performed, which tickets are escalated, and which policy guidance employees receive. This affects job functions, workload, and potentially employment outcomes.

## AI-SE role ownership

### Record-keeping / logging (Art.12)
Based on the EU AI Act Art.12 AI-SE must ensure the audit log schema is present, log retention policy are applied, tamper-evident logs are recorded. 

### Technical documentation (Art.11)
Based on the EU AI Act Art.11 AI-SE must ensure the technical documentation is kept up to date: fully versioned documentation per release; architecture diagrams; dependency inventory; interface contracts.
